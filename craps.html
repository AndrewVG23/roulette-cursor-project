How to play

Tap the felt to drop chips. Aim with the arrow keys, hold Space (or the throw button) to charge, then release to hurl the dice — they bounce off the felt and the far wall.

  • ← → — Aim left / right toward the far wall.
  • ↑ ↓ — More loft / flatter throw.
  • Hold Space — Charge power; release to throw.
  • Pass / Don't Pass / Odds / Come / Field / Props — Standard craps action.
